What exactly is our Role?

01

Administration Duties

The Human Resource Department will be able to act as the liaison between teachers, staff and school administrators. Including:

Capital

    Assigning the appropriate amount of salary based on their work performance.

Correspondence:

    Internally:

      1. Managing official files of curriculum vitaes and related documents
      2. Managing interviews and appointments for potential employees

    Externally:

      Updating on available post in Politeknik Brunei to Public Service Comission

Recruitment:

    Contacting and communicate with recruitment agencies for scouting out potential employees.

Rank promotion:

    1. Determine if an employee is worthy of granting a higher rank promotion in office based on their work performance
    2. A high ranking employee will be able to be elected as Head of a certain position.

Evaluation:

    Determine if the employee is able to keep up the work that is expected from Politeknik Brunei

02

Human Resource Management

1. Scouting for trainers outside and within the country border
2. Communicate with the trainers for potential contract
3. Acquiring and share solid training-related information to relevant departments of Politeknik Brunei
4. Assign suitable employee as training officers and staffs
5. Acquiring approval for training application from the Permanent Secretary of Higher Education regarding:

    a. Accommodation

    b. Transportation

    c. Programme Fee

6. Issuing feedback forms for employees that will be having a training programme
7. Manage long-term training/meeting/seminar/conference locally and abroad (In-Service Training):

    Circulate information about the courses offered and their datelines.

    Review application forms.

    Establish an Ad Hoc committee to shortlist the candidates.

    Extend application to the Ministry of Education.

    Provide feedback on the outcome of the application.
