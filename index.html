    How does our Human Resource Department works?

    Human Resource Department is designed to manage people in Politeknik Brunei. Human resource management is like a cogwheel, requiring the teamwork of everyone to keep Politeknik Brunei function as an educational institution.

    How does it related with work?

    This Department ensure the organization to connects mission with the work of employees. Thus, synchronizing the work flow of Politeknik Brunei.

    Is it safe?

    Human Resource Department will always ensure the safety, health and welfare of employee at work. As having a good health condition of an employee is more beneficial and productive.
